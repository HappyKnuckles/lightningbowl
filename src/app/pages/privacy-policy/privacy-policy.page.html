Privacy Policy Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2025

1. Introduction

Lightning Bowl ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and store your information when you use our application.

2. Data We Collect

Lightning Bowl collects the following minimal data:

  • OAuth access token: When you sign in via OAuth, we receive and store only an access token on our backend. We do not receive or store your email address, password, or any other account information.
  • Anonymous analytics: We collect anonymous usage analytics that contain no personal or user-identifiable information.

3. Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect or store:

  • Your email address or password
  • Your bowling scores, game history, league data, or equipment information â€” all of this stays on your device
  • Any other personal data

4. How We Use OAuth

OAuth is used exclusively for one-time authentication to verify your identity. After sign-in, only an access token is stored on our backend solely to enable cloud synchronization features. We do not use OAuth tokens for any other purpose and do not share them with third parties.

5. Local Data Storage

All bowling data (scores, game history, league data, equipment, etc.) is stored exclusively on your device using local storage. None of this data is transmitted to or stored on our servers.

6. Analytics

We collect anonymous analytics to help improve the app. This data is fully anonymised and cannot be used to identify any individual user.

7. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your data to third parties.

8. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via the feedback form in the Settings page.
